Achievements of Real Madrid

Record-Breaking Success

Real Madrid's achievements are a testament to their dedication to success and love for the game. Here’s a look at some of the milestones that make Real Madrid one of the most celebrated clubs in history.

"The TOUR BERNABÉU!"

Notable Achievements:

35 La Liga Titles

Real Madrid holds the record for the highest number of La Liga titles, with their most recent victory in May 2024, showcasing their remarkable consistency and dominance in Spanish football. Under the management of Carlo Ancelotti, the team displayed exceptional performances throughout the season, highlighted by contributions from stars like Vinícius Júnior and Jude Bellingham.

15 UEFA Champions League Titles

The club also boasts the most Champions League titles, with 15 victories to its name. Their latest triumph came in June 2024, when they defeated Manchester City 3-2 in a thrilling final, further cementing Real Madrid’s legacy as a powerhouse in European football. This win underscores their unparalleled history in the tournament, having consistently excelled on the continental stage.

5 Intercontinental Cups and 4 FIFA Club World Cups

Real Madrid has proven its strength on the world stage by winning 5 Intercontinental Cups and 4 FIFA Club World Cups, reflecting their success beyond European competitions. These titles highlight the club's ability to compete and succeed against top teams from other continents, further enhancing their reputation as one of the best football clubs globally.

13 Spanish Super Cups

In addition to their league and international titles, Real Madrid has won the Spanish Super Cup 13 times, with their latest victory coming in January 2024. This achievement showcases the club’s consistent performance in domestic competitions and their ability to compete at the highest level.

Successful Signings

In the summer of 2024, Real Madrid completed the signing of French superstar Kylian Mbappé, regarded as one of the best players in the world. This transfer came after years of attempts and waiting, as Mbappé joined Madrid on a free transfer from Paris Saint-Germain, expected to provide a significant boost to the team's attacking line with high expectations for his impact in upcoming competitions. Additionally, Real Madrid has strengthened its squad with other successful signings, including Jude Bellingham, who joined in the summer of 2023 and has delivered outstanding performances in midfield, and Vinícius Júnior, who has emerged as a rising star and is considered a key element in the attack.

Historical Recognition

Real Madrid is not just a collection of titles; it embodies a rich history of legendary players, unforgettable moments, and a passionate fan base. The club has produced some of the greatest talents in football history, including Alfredo Di Stéfano, Ferenc Puskás, Raúl, Zinedine Zidane, Cristiano Ronaldo, and many more.

Global Brand

Beyond its achievements on the pitch, Real Madrid has built a global brand, with millions of fans worldwide. Their iconic white jersey, known as "Los Blancos," represents excellence, history, and tradition, resonating with supporters across the globe.
